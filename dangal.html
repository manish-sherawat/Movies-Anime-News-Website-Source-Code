Dangal

Dangal is a biographical sports drama that follows the journey of Mahavir Singh Phogat, who trains his daughters to become world-class wrestlers, challenging societal norms and gender stereotypes.

Plot Synopsis

Mahavir Singh Phogat, a former wrestler, dreams of winning gold for India in wrestling. He trains his daughters, Geeta and Babita, to achieve this dream, facing numerous challenges along the way.

Key Characters

  • Mahavir Singh Phogat: The father and coach, determined to train his daughters.
  • Geeta Phogat: The elder daughter, who becomes India's first female wrestler to win a gold medal at the Commonwealth Games.
  • Babita Phogat: The younger daughter, who also excels in wrestling.

Themes and Style

  • Empowerment: The film highlights women's empowerment and breaking gender barriers.
  • Family: The bond between father and daughters is central to the story.
  • Perseverance: The struggle and determination required to achieve success.
