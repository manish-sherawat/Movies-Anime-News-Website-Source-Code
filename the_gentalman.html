The Gentleman: A Netflix Series Deep Dive into Aristocratic Crime

Dive into the stylish and dangerous world of "The Gentleman," a captivating Netflix series. Inspired by the Guy Ritchie film of the same name, this series delivers a fresh take on the intersection of aristocracy and organized crime, filled with sharp wit, intricate plots, and memorable characters.

Plot Synopsis

When Eddie Horniman inherits his father's sizable estate, he discovers that it is part of a cannabis empire run by the Glass family. Moreover, Eddie finds himself navigating a world of crime, dangerous figures, and unexpected alliances as he tries to protect his family and their estate.

Key Characters

  • Eddie Horniman: A soldier who inherits his father's estate and finds himself embroiled in criminal activities.
  • Susie Glass: A smart and business-savvy woman who runs the cannabis operation associated with the Horniman estate.
